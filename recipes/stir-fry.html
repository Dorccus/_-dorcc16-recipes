Chicken Stir-Fry

Chicken breasts and chicken thighs both work well for stir fries.

Ingredients

Just make sure the pieces are relatively small and thin. Plus, they should be about the same size so the chicken cooks evenly.

This chicken stir-fry recipe is packed with veggies and is quick and easy to prepare. Try adding bean sprouts, bamboo shoots, snap peas, or any of your favorite vegetables. Serve it with white or brown rice, or noodles.
